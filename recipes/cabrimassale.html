Cabri Massale

Description

The Cabri Massalé is the most representative dish of Tamil ”Malabar” cuisine on Reunion Island.

Ingredients

  • 1,5kg of Cabri
  • 4 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 large can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tamarind paste
  • 1 half inch of ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of turmeric
  • 3 teaspoons of massalé
  • 3 teaspoons of coarse salt

Steps

  1. Start by pouring 5 dl of water into a bowl and add the tamarind to dilute.
  2. Cut the meat into small pieces. Chop the onions and mash the garlic, coarse salt and ginger.
  3. Pour a drizzle of oil into a pot and put the meat to cook.
  4. When the meat becomes well colored, add the onions and leave for about 2 minutes until the onions are colored.
  5. Add the garlic, and cook for about 3 minutes.
  6. Add the tomato and let return for 5 min.
  7. Add the massalé and turmeric.
  8. Cover the meat with water and simmer over low heat for about 1 hour.
  9. Add the tamarind after an hour of cooking the goat massale, mix and cook for 10 minutes. Serve hot with white rice and a “good” Larson broth.
