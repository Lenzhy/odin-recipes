Cabri Massale

Description

The Cabri Massalé is the most representative dish of Tamil ”Malabar” cuisine on Reunion Island.

Ingredients

  • 1,5kg of Cabri
  • 4 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 large can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of tamarind paste
  • 1 half inch of ginger
  • 2 teaspoons of turmeric

Steps

  1. If the sausages aren’t too salty boil them once. Otherwise do it 2 or 3 times while piercing them with a fork (5 minutes).
  2. Rinse. Cook the sausages again for 10 minutes in a little water until the water has totally disappeared.
  3. Fry them with the 3 spoons of oil.
  4. Add the onions cut lengthwise, turn well, then add the crushed pepper and salt. Let brown.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes
  6. Cover and cook for 15 to 20 minutes
